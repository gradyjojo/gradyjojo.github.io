Business Insight

Overview

Here is the section where we will provide insights that we have gathered through the data analysis process.

The insights that we present are in the form of recommendations that are appropriate for each region, with reference to how high or low the CLV numbers are in each region. If a region has a high CLV number, then it will be recommended to implement a strategy in the form of increasing revenue. Meanwhile, if a region has a low CLV number, then it will be recommended to implement strategies such as reducing costs.

Overall Insight

  • Profit Growth: Profit is projected to increase by 13.14% in 2018.

  • Impact of Discounts: Discounts have proven to be highly effective in attracting new customers, with 97.73% of new Customers influenced by promotional discounts.

  • Strategy for High CLV Regions: In regions with high Customer Lifetime Value (CLV), implementing a revenue increase strategy is more effective. Offering discounts can attract new customers and retain existing ones, therby boosting overall profits.

  • Strategy for Low CLV Regions: In regions with low CLV, a cost reduction strategy is more suitable. Large discounts may not be as relevant because they tend to increase expenses. Therefore, it is advisable to reduce the frequency and intensity of discounts in these areas.

Recommendations

  • Focus on Key Categories: Continue to prioritize the office supplies and furniture categories, as these contribute significantly to Giant's revenue.

  • Adjust Discount Strategy: Reduce the frequency and intensity of discounts on binders, especially in the southern and central regions, to optimize cost management.
